All about Me

My name is Anne, and I am currently majoring in Foreign Languages and Literature at NTHU.

My hobbies are listening to music, watching movies and traveling to different countries. I enjoy a wide range of genres, and the one I listen to most is pop, K-pop to be specific. I have been a fan of Seventeen since 2018, captivated by their synchronized performances.

As I explored further, my appreciation for the Korean music has also expanded to include a band called “The Rose”. While the former brings me dynamic energy, the latter fascinates me with their soulful and emotive music. These two groups also symbolize the diversity of my playlists.

Aside from my hobbies, I also do other meaningful things in my free time. For instance, I have been a volunteer to teach kids at the children’s home with other students in our university for three semesters. Moreover, I have also worked as a tutor at a cram school as a part time job. The reason I chose to do so is because I genuinely enjoy spending time with children and am also considering a career in education. Therefore, I try to have more experience in teaching and interacting with children. To me, teaching is not just about sharing knowledge, but about broadening horizons for children—just as music and travel have done for me. As someone who embraces diversity and loves trying new things, I look forward to bringing this curiosity and energy into my future career in education.
